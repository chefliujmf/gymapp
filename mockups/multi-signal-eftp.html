How the weighting works (not shown to the user)
Each signal returns a threshold estimate + a reliability. Weight = base-reliability × freshness decay.
• test-backed (CP, 20-min) = high  • physiological (HR-power, drift, HRV) = medium
• a signal that disagrees >10% is DOWN-weighted (likely bad data), never silently averaged in.
Output = weighted mean + a band from the spread; confidence = how tightly they agree. All pure + unit-tested.
