Verdict

Platyplus is a genuinely good coach for its two well-trodden personas (male cyclist JM, pregnant runner Xenia) and its readiness engine is world-class (individualized, evidence-based, code-enforced at save). But against the world-class bar — code-enforced-at-save for anything structural/safety, universal across all personas, safe-by-construction — it is still a 3-out-of-5 product: enforcement is the weakest dimension (2.95/5) because most structural and even several SAFETY rules (teen loading, postpartum impact, concurrent-training separation, pregnancy interval-structure, weekly volume for every strength/tri/swim persona) remain prompt-only or detection-only, and the two most exposed personas (a performance teen with no DOB, a raw beginner with an empty goal) are actively defaulted INTO an adult VO2 build with no safe fallback. This is a modest, real improvement over the prior 62/100 — the shape clamp, pregnancy sweep, gym rep clamp and readiness math are new hard code — but it is not the leap the code comments claim (several say "CODE-ENFORCED" for things that only reach a prompt), and the theme layer regressed into an unenforceable state where the mandated --amber/--cyan/--warn tokens do not even exist in :root.

What regressed vs 62

1) Theme palette is now systemically UNENFORCEABLE: the tokens the "always apply tokens" rule names (--amber/--cyan/--warn/--info) are NOT defined in styles.css :root (only --accent/--danger/--text/--line), so 20+ screens hardcode raw hex by necessity and every var(--warn,#…) silently resolves to its fallback — the off-theme-blue defect class the memory flags is now everywhere (readiness #7a8cff, gym cyan-vs-indigo, run zones, admin, report pill). 2) Stale post-cut copy leaked: after Eat/Mind was dropped (#488/#491) the chat empty-state still promotes "plan or adjust your training & meals" + a "Plan 3 high-protein dinners" chip that the server 409s — a dead-end on 100% of first chats. 3) Comment/behaviour DRIFT where enforcement is claimed but absent: the #719 "CODE-ENFORCED on the actual TSS targets" comment (cycle load) and weeklyLoadBudget both overstate — the number only reaches a prompt; no save-time TSS clamp exists.

Genuine strengths

  • Readiness engine is genuinely world-class: Sleep/Freshness/Energy derived per-athlete from intervals wellness, evidence-based, and CODE-enforced (Check-in audit scored 4/5/5/5/4) — the standout of the app
  • Pregnancy is near-safe-by-construction: maintenance shape, tempo/endurance ceiling, supine gym swap, and absolute privacy scrub are all code-enforced at save AND swept (reenforceShapeAll), not left to the prompt
  • A real code-enforced shape clamp exists for ride/run: enforceShape/enforceShapeIntensity/reenforceShapeAll clamp per-segment intensity % and the weekly quality-day COUNT, and sweep stale sessions from earlier runs
  • Gym engine has concrete hard code: clampMainReps (rep scheme), an enforceTeenGym rep floor, and sex/goal-adaptive split + assembler machinery — structure the LLM authors within rather than invents
  • Cycling and running stats are truly individualized: the user's own threshold power / VDOT drive zones and race predictions, and the run path reads per-user threshold pace and prompts when unset
  • Coach review flow is code-enforced and robust: feedback-gated reviews with async poll + retry, deterministic ids
  • Menstrual handling beats most apps: the menses green-light is preserved and readiness is phase-corrected in code (not just prose)
  • Plan surface is rich and universal across sports/personas with solid empty and loading states, and Postgres-backed per-user data with per-user intervals-athlete isolation is sound
