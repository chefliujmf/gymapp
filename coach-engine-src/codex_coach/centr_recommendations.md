# Centr Recommendations

These are Centr selections that fit the cycling plan. The coach plan remains the source of truth for load and timing.

## Current Best Match

### HYROX Recover

Use for:
- transition week after Girona
- rain-day gym fallback
- recovery days between outdoor rides
- mobility before or after light strength

Why it fits:
- recovery-oriented
- includes mobility
- low volume
- bodyweight mobility sessions are only 20 minutes
- less likely to create soreness than strength, HIIT, HIRT, or hybrid programs

Selected sessions:
- `HYROX Recover - Week 1 Day 1 - Mobility - 20 min - Bodyweight`
- `HYROX Recover - Week 1 Day 3 - Mobility - 20 min - Bodyweight`

How to use:
- On a gym day, do the Centr mobility session first if you feel stiff.
- If legs are heavy, do only the Centr mobility session and skip lower-body strength.
- If legs feel good, do the planned light strength table after the mobility session.
- Keep the total session at least 45 minutes by adding easy cardio, breathing, and light trunk work.

## Secondary Options

### Strength for Sports / Multi-Gym Strength

Use for:
- summer strength-maintenance gym day
- weeks where the goal is one supportive full-gym session, not hypertrophy
- exercise demonstrations for squat, hinge, pull, single-leg, and trunk patterns

Why it fits:
- closer to the coach-prescribed movement patterns than yoga or Pilates
- more gym-specific than HYROX Recover
- useful when full gym equipment is available

How to use:
- pick the closest Centr workout that includes a squat or leg press, Romanian deadlift or hinge, row or lat pulldown, split squat or step-up, and core;
- keep only the exercises that match the coach plan if the Centr workout is too long or too bodybuilding-focused;
- cap effort at RPE 5-6 with 2-3 reps in reserve;
- skip hard finishers, HIIT, or conditioning sections.

Current limitation:
- Centr exposed program names but not a definitive single workout title through the collector. Treat `Strength for Sports` and `Multi-Gym Strength` as **unverified** program names to inspect in-app on gym day (per "Source Verification" in `source_library_workflow.md`) — present them to the athlete labeled as unverified, not as confirmed links.

### Centr Pilates Fusion

Use sparingly for:
- deload week
- flexibility and trunk control
- days with no bike intensity nearby

Best visible candidate:
- `Centr Pilates Fusion - Week 1 Day 4 - Pilates & mobility - 45 min`

Do not use this if:
- legs are sore
- sleep was poor
- a bike quality day is planned within 24 hours

### Korey Rowe: Strength in Motion

Not selected for the current transition week.

Reason:
- includes strength, plyometric HIIT, and mobility
- better suited later when fatigue is controlled
- too risky immediately after Girona

## Avoid For Current Phase Unless Explicitly Planned

- HIIT
- HIRT
- hybrid conditioning
- glute hypertrophy
- heavy lower-body strength
- hard Da Rulk functional conditioning
- anything that makes the next outdoor ride worse
