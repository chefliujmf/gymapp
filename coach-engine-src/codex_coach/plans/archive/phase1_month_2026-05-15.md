## Book-Derived Load Guardrails

- Use TSS rather than hours alone when comparing load, because it captures duration and intensity.
- After the Girona block, use a true transition week around 4-5 bike hours and roughly 125-160 bike TSS.
- Build weeks should return toward 5.5-7.5 bike hours and roughly 250-320 bike TSS when the Intervals fitness graph would otherwise stay gray or positive/fresh.
- An opportunity week can reach 7-8 hours only if sleep, RPE, Feel, and weather are all favorable.
- Do not plan 10-hour weeks in this phase; reserve that for a deliberate overload block after several stable weeks.
- Keep about 70-80% of riding time easy, mostly below aerobic threshold / in endurance zones.
- Do not make the plan zone 2 only; use one purposeful intensity session when recovery supports it.
- Strength is maintenance in summer build weeks: one gym session is the default unless strength becomes a limiter or weather forces a one-off substitution.
- Four bike rides is the default build-week structure; a fifth ride is optional only when there is a clean 60-minute window and it helps consistency or moves build weeks into controlled green form without creating fatigue debt. If only 30-50 minutes are available, use rest, mobility, or a non-debt support note unless the athlete explicitly accepts a short ride.
- Green-zone target for build weeks is roughly `-10` to `-25` form after the main weekly load anchor, but do not chase green if RPE, Feel, sleep, calendar, or weather are poor. If projected Form rises through a supposed build block, the block is underloaded unless a deliberate recovery/taper is intended.
- Friday PM is a high-value summer bike window. Use it for outdoor riding when dry; place gym on a lower-value weekday unless Friday weather or family schedule blocks riding.

## Adaptation Rules

- If sleep is poor or dinner/bedtime runs late, downgrade the next day by one level: intensity to endurance, endurance to recovery, recovery to rest.
- If rain blocks outdoor riding, do not use the indoor trainer in summer. Move the ride or use gym/yoga/mobility.
- If subjective RPE is 2 points higher than expected, cut volume before cutting frequency.
- If two warning signs appear together, remove intensity for 48 hours: poor sleep, heavy legs, elevated stress, irritability, unusual soreness, unusually high HR for easy power.
- If all recovery markers are good for 7-10 days, progress duration before progressing intensity.
