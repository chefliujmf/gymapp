# New Athlete Onboarding Questionnaire

Engine-level, reusable for any new person (see the multi-user engine note). Answers seed that
person's isolated data: `athlete_profile`, objectives/target macro, `ftp_estimate`, plan
constraints, and communication style. Keep it light — "don't know" is fine for power numbers;
the coach estimates from rides and refines. ⭐ = needed to start; the rest sharpen the plan.

## 1. About you
- ⭐ Name / what to call you
- ⭐ Age (or date of birth)
- ⭐ Height and weight — and do you prefer **metric or imperial**?
- Sex (used for physiology and W/kg framing)

## 2. Your goal
- ⭐ In a sentence or two: what do you want out of cycling?
- ⭐ Main objective + any target date (e.g. raise FTP, finish a gran fondo, climb better, general fitness, lose weight)
- Rider you want to become: **sustained/diesel**, **punchy/sprinter**, or **all-round**?
- Any events on the calendar (date + type)?

## 3. Current fitness
- ⭐ How long have you trained with structure?
- ⭐ Current FTP if you know it — and how you know it (recent test / app estimate / guess)?
- ⭐ Typical riding lately: hours/week (and weekly TSS if you track it)
- Any recent best efforts you remember (e.g. 20-min or 1-hour power)?
- Willing to do an occasional test or hard validation effort when it's useful? (yes/no)

## 4. Time & availability
- ⭐ Weekday time you can usually train (per day)
- ⭐ Weekend time per day, and which day(s) can hold a **long ride**
- Realistic **max** weekly hours
- Hard constraints to plan around (work shifts, family/kids, regular travel, a second home/cottage)

## 5. Equipment & platforms
- ⭐ Bikes you have (road / gravel / TT) and a **smart trainer**?
- ⭐ Power meter? HR strap? Head unit (Wahoo / Garmin)?
- Gym access (full gym / home setup / none) and do you want strength in the plan?
- ⭐ Accounts so I can read data and publish workouts: **intervals.icu** (athlete id + API key), Strava, Platyplus?
- Watch/wearable for sleep & HRV?

## 6. Health & recovery
- ⭐ Injuries, recurring niggles, or movement limits I should respect
- Health conditions or meds that affect training (optional, only what's relevant)
- Typical sleep (hours) and current life-stress level (low / medium / high)
- Anything that regularly disrupts training (travel, seasons, weather, schedule)

## 7. Nutrition
- ⭐ Diet style (omnivore / vegetarian / vegan / other) and any allergies
- Do you fuel during rides now? (gels / real food / just water)
- Supplements you take
- Weight goal, if any (lose / maintain / gain) — only if it matters to you

## 8. How you like to be coached
- ⭐ Prefer **one clear call**, or a couple of **options** to choose from?
- Tone: direct/blunt or encouraging/gentle?
- How much "why" do you want behind each session — brief or detailed?
- Public ride titles/descriptions (Strava): any style you like, and anything off-limits to share publicly?
